ABOUT ME

Why COMPUTER SCIENCE?

The first time I heard of computer science was during enrollment. The course that I was to pursue was Multi-Media Arts/MMA, exploring the world of graphic design, Animation, Video Editing, etc. but MMA is a bit fancy for me. A friend of mine invited me to enroll in DLSJBC as a CS student at that time I didn’t know what is computer science so I searched “What is Computer Science?” and found out that computer science is the study of computers and computational systems. So why computer science? I studied computer science because I wanted to learn how to build and design software applications. I've always been interested in creating things and I saw computer science as a way to turn that passion into a career. I also wanted to work in a field that is in high demand and offers a good salary.
